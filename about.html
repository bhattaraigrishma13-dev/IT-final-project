About Us

About No Diet With Grishma

Welcome to No Diet With Grishma — a food blog dedicated to celebrating the rich and delicious food culture of Nepal. We believe that great food brings people together, and no one should hold back when it comes to enjoying a good meal!

From steaming momos to hearty Thakali sets, we cover the best Nepali dishes and the stories behind them. Our mission is simple: find the most delicious food and share it with everyone.

Whether you are a local foodie or a tourist exploring Kathmandu, we are here to guide you to the tastiest meals Nepal has to offer.

Meet Grishma

The Founder

Grishma is a passionate food lover from Kathmandu who started this blog with one simple idea — life is too short to say no to good food. With a deep love for Nepali cuisine, she set out to explore every corner of the food scene.

She believes in honest reviews, real recommendations, and most importantly, eating without guilt. Her motto: "Calories don't count when the food is this good!"
